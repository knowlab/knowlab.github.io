#### UK National Health Services
We work closely with National Health Service (NHS) across the UK to use our [health informatics tools](/tools) for improving services and clinical research, including [South London and Maudsley NHS Trust](https://www.slam.nhs.uk/), [King's College Hospital NHS Foundation Trust](https://www.kch.nhs.uk/), [University College London Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust](https://www.uclh.nhs.uk/Pages/Home.aspx), [NHS Lothian](https://www.nhslothian.scot/Pages/default.aspx), [NHS Education for Scotland](https://www.nhslothian.scot/Pages/default.aspx), and [eDRIS of Public Health Scotland](https://www.isdscotland.org/products-and-services/edris/).
