KnowLab is a health informatics research group, aiming at improving healthcare by artificial intelligence technologies. Our research focuses are machine learning, text analytics, knowledge graph, and their applications in health care. We are based in University College London and University of Edinburgh.

We closely work with National Health Services across the UK by using our [health informatics tools](/tools) for improving services and clinical research. Just name a few: [South London and Maudsley NHS Trust](https://www.slam.nhs.uk/), [King's College Hospital NHS Foundation Trust](https://www.kch.nhs.uk/), [University College London Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust](https://www.uclh.nhs.uk/Pages/Home.aspx), [NHS Lothian](https://www.nhslothian.scot/Pages/default.aspx), and [NHS Education for Scotland](https://www.nhslothian.scot/Pages/default.aspx).
