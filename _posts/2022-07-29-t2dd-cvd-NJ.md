title: Our collaboration study titled "Prediction of Five-Year Cardiovascular Disease Risk in People with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus - Derivation in Nanjing, China and External Validation in Scotland, UK", led by [Cheng Wan](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Cheng-Wan-3) from Nanjing Medical University, has been published by [Global Heart](https://globalheartjournal.com/article/10.5334/gh.1131/). This study shows it is feasible to generate a risk prediction model using routinely collected Chinese hospital data. This indicates there is a great potential to make use of the large-scale and relatively easy accessible route data for identifying those at risk of CVD and help significantly improve CVD prevention in people with diabetes.
