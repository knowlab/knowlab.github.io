title: Clinical coding is the task of transforming medical information in a patient's health records into structured codes like ICD-10 for diagnosis, which is cognitive, time-consuming task and error-prone. In this preprint, titled [*Automated Clinical Coding - What, Why, and Where We Are?*](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2203.11092) , Hang introduces the idea of automated clinical coding and summarises its challenges from the perspective of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP), based on the literature, our project experience over the past two and half years (late 2019 - early 2022), and discussions with clinical coding experts in Scotland and the UK.
