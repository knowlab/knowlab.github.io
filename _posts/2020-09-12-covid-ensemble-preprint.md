title: Our study uses **ensemble learning**  to synergise seven multinational prediction models to realise a robust and high-performing prediction model. This is the first work to use ensemble learning for risk prediction of COVID-19 and the validation cohorts are one of the most diverse international COVID-19 datasets (4 cohorts with mortality rates 2.4-45%). The ensemble model consistently outperformed any single models in all aspects validated. [preprint](http://doi.org/10.13140/RG.2.2.15484.97927), [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/Honghan/EnsemblePrediction)
