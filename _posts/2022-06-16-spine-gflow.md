title: Our collaboration study titled "Spine-GFlow - A Hybrid Learning Framework for Robust Multi-tissue Segmentation in Lumbar MRI without Manual Annotation", led by [Dr Teng Zhang](https://www.aimed.hku.hk/teng-zhang) from Hong Kong University, has been accepted by [Computerized Medical Imaging and Graphics](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0895611122000647). Results of this study show that our method, without requiring manual annotation, has achieved a segmentation performance comparable to a model trained with full supervision (mean Dice 0.914 vs 0.916).
